Negroni

Description

This Italian cocktail will knock your socks off!

Ingredients

  • Campari
  • Gin
  • Vermouth
  • Carbonated Water
  • 1 lemon wedge for garnish

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a shaker
  2. Pour into a negroni glass
  3. Garnish with lemon wedge and Enjoy!
