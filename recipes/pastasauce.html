Pasta Sauce

Description

This Italian pasta sauce recipe will make a great weekend or date night dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 28 can crushed tomoatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a sauce pan
  2. Simmer on low for 1 hour
